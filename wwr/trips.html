Included Equipment

All our trips include professional-grade equipment to ensure safety and comfort on the river:

  • Life Jackets: U.S. Coast Guard approved buoyancy vests.
  • Helmets: Lightweight yet strong for impact protection.
  • Paddles: Durable, adjustable paddles for different water levels.
  • Rafts: Non-slip, reinforced inflatable rafts for up to 8 people.
  • Wetsuits & Booties: For cold-water comfort and insulation.
