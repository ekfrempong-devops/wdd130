Ghana

Ghana is a country in West Africa located on the Gulf of Guinea, known for its tropical climate, diverse landscape including rainforests and savanna, and rich cultural heritage. As the first sub-Saharan nation to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957, it has evolved into a multi-party democracy with a growing economy, primarily driven by natural resources like gold, cocoa, and oil. The capital is Accra, and the country is characterized by its warm, welcoming population and vibrant cultural scene
